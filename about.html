About Us

Founded in fall 2017, we have spent months focusing and refining the most prized piece of clothing in the American fashion canon: the button-down. For centuries, Americans have worn button-downs without considering its aged look and its dated style. They have ignored the stagnation and monotony of a clothing so integral to Western fashion. zenith is here to change that.

Through countless studies and hours of grueling research, our team identified the shortcomings of the button-down and crafted a newer, better button-down for Americans to wear.

We believe our collections represent the best of fashion, if not the best of America.
